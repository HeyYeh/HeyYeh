Welcome to my portfolio!

This is a nice little sample collection of videos that I've worked on, ranging across all of my roles. Writing is the consistent job I've nabbed, but I've been lucky enough to dabble in being a Producer, Art Director, Assistant Director, and Production Designer too. I wear many hats...

I've Been Blind - The Fratellis

An iTunes number 1 video about the pursuit of colour in a colourless world

Totem - How's Harry

An experimental video taking a shot at the constructed nature of music videos. Meta, right?

Do Yourself A Favor - Kid Creole

A departure from my usual style, trying out a more glossy and straightforward story about jealousy.

Seraphim - Music Video

An incredibly dark video featuring terrifying creatures, working angel wings, and a large tree inside a warehouse.

One Foot In This Club - Music Video

A band kidnapping, more rope than you can shake a stick at, and a man headbutting himself... It's an interesting music video!

Breathe - Music Video

A video featuring a host of smoke based practical effects, haunting imagery, and some truly beautiful cinematic shots.

I Am God, and Severely Underqualified - Short

A philosophical look at a writer, trying to find control in a world that he's creating - currently on the film festical circuit.

Earthquake - Music Video

A raw, home video-esque project following a day-in-the life of an indie rock band

Where did time go? - Music Video

A colourful video that pokes fun at that traditional (weird) artsy music video! Featuring a bath full of fruit, confetti blood, and more!

Amber - Short

My first film project, and a psychological thriller following the unusual aftermath of a home invasion...
