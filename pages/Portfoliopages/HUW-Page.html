Held Under Water - Phoebe Katis

Behind the video:

This is another one of the early ones, and I have less to say on this one purely because of the passage of time and how memory works. That isn’t all bad in this case though, because the heart and soul of the video is something to enjoy visually rather than through my occasionally okay jokes…

This one was a fun production period throughout the whole process, from concept to delivery. The actual video was inspired by meeting someone with a mild synaesthesia for music, meaning that different musical notes made them literally see different colours. It’s an incredibly interesting condition with some people sourcing it right down to grey matter, which is some deep brain stuff, but… But this is a video post and I have to control myself. Ahem. Anyway, we sourced four artists of different styles, gave them a giant canvas, strapped cameras to their heads, and played the song on repeat at them until they either submitted or painted the whole canvas. Luckily the canvas was painted before anyone tapped out.

It is a simple concept, but it turned into a really beautiful end product, and the painting was eventually split into four and each artist went home with a pretty little quarter. Happy endings, am I right?
