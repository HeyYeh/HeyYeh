Balloon Girl

Behind the stitch:

Another stitch-ified Banksy piece, except this was made on request by a family member and given as a gift! This is possible even more famous than Flower Thrower, and was, according to a poll by Samsung, apparently even voted the UK’s favourite art piece in general. For the world of simple pixel art designs though, this was just another fun look into using silhouette and shape to make a nice piece for a living room wall! This was, like a lot of the stuff I do, designed in Pyxel first and any little additions, like adding a grid or tracking the stitches that I’ve done, was done in Gimp.

The only major thing that separates this from the other stitch-ified Banksy stuff that I have cobbled together is the sheer size of it; it is double the size of Flower Thrower! This was for two reasons: first of all (most importantly of all), because I was asked to make it relatively large so that it looks nice on a living room wall. Secondly, making the piece bigger means that I can push more detail into shaping it, and, when you’re trying to draw a face in little squares, the more wiggle room you have with detail the better. This was a very fun piece to do either way, and, though there are some improvements that could be made, both me and the giftee were happy with it, and that’s what really matters. As with anything here, feel free to make this pattern yourself, and please give me some kind of shout out and show me what you make!
