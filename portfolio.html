WHO DO I THINK I AM?

I'm a London based writer and filmmaker with experience in brand strategy, copywriting, more than my fair share of video work, and I'm pretty nifty with a 3D printer.

I'm lucky enough to be part of the directing duo Bousher & Gee, otherwise known as The Ginger and the Jew.

Reviews for my work tend to go along the lines of "some nice dark comedy there", "such fascinating scenarios", and "oh God, why?"
